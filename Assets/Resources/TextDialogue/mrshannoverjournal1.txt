July 8, 1963
Three days after bringing Oscar home and he is already missing. As with the rabbits, cats, and birds before him we fear the worst. 
Mr. Hannover is more confident than I am that Daisy needs special care. He is assured that she isn't a normal girl. 
This hurts my soul because she is my little Daisy. My little girl. Yet I cannot ignore the facts. 
If there is something that can help my little girl . . . I hope something can help her. 
...
Ms. Hannover
